About us

Implementing One-Stop Solution for eradicating mosquito-borne diseases

M-zap is powered by artificial intelligence that can classify mosquito species,
predict malaria cases in an area and visualize malaria hotspots. Our web-based application conveys state-of-the-art
AI strategies to field workers and common people simplistically, encouraging a wide range of adoption.

Who we are

We are Harsh, Aditya, and Akhil, the students of NIT Hamirpur, pursuing Btech. We have a common interest in the field of research and innovation. We met at a national-level hackathon conducted by our university, we collaborated and won it. The team evolved with a matching vision of two individuals who are learning, just to create a positive effect in the lives of people on this earth.

What drives us

We three are driven by passion and looking forward to making a positive impact on this world through our skills. We are inspired to harness AI for controlling mosquito-borne diseases.
